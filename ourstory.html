OCAKES HISTORY

Ocakes Bakeshop began as a small home-based business, founded by someone with a deep love for baking and a dream of turning that passion into something bigger. It all started in a tiny kitchen, where family recipes were transformed into cakes and pastries shared with neighbors and friends. Word of mouth quickly spread, and what began as a hobby grew into a full-fledged bakeshop. Over the years, Ocakes Bakeshop has become a place where people can celebrate special occasions, create lasting memories, and enjoy treats made with care and dedication

MILESTONES

Throughout our journey, we’ve achieved several milestones that have shaped who we are today. These achievements are a reflection of the trust and love from our customers, and we look forward to creating even more delicious memories with you. Thank you for being part of the Ocakes journey!

  • ⦁ Winning the "Best New Bakery Award" in our first year of operation (2020).
  • ⦁ Reaching 10,000 cakes sold within two years.
  • ⦁ Opening three additional branches to bring Ocakes closer to our customers--SM Dasmarinas, SM Molino, and SM Bacoor.
  • ⦁ Launching our “Seasonal Specials,” which quickly became customer favorites.
  • ⦁ Partnering with a local charity (Care for Paws Shelter Sanctuary) to donate a portion of our profits to community projects.
  • ⦁ Receiving the “Customer Choice Award” for three consecutive years (2023).
  • ⦁ Hosting a highly successful “Baking for Beginners” workshop, attended by over 100 participants (2022).
  • ⦁ Introducing our first-ever international-inspired dessert line, which was featured in The New York Times, food and lifestyle magazine (2023).
